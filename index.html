About Matrix Bond

Built on Science.
Driven by Performance.

Matrix Bond is a technology-forward structural bonding company specializing in high-performance adhesive systems, composite joining, and surface preparation for aerospace, automotive, energy, and heavy industrial sectors.

Our proprietary bonding matrices are formulated to withstand extreme temperatures, vibration, chemical exposure, and mechanical stress — making us the partner of choice when failure is not an option.

  • Epoxy, polyurethane & hybrid adhesive systems
  • Composite-to-metal & composite-to-composite bonding
  • Surface treatment & pre-bond preparation
  • Structural repair & rehabilitation programs
  • Custom formulation & laboratory testing
